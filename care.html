Sending care packages is a thoughtful way for loved ones to provide much-needed supplies to deployed service members. However, the cost of collecting items for care packages can add up quickly. But with some creativity and the discounts we offer, you can send care packages without breaking the bank. Below are categories for different types of care packages and their estimated costs of supplies:

Category A  USD($1500)
Provide Entertainment: Service members can always use some entertainment during their downtime. This package can include items such as books, magazines, crosswords, puzzles, earbuds, games, batteries, DVDs, card games, and power banks.

Category B  USD($1200)
Drink Mixes and Edibles: Drink mixes can offer a quick and easy way for service members to enjoy something other than water during deployment. This package can include hot cocoa, instant coffee, tea bags, creamer, lemonade, iced tea, as well as easy protein snacks like energy bars, granola bars, tuna fish, beef jerky, summer sausage, Slim Jim sticks, and canned chili. Easy condiments like hot sauce, relish, mustard, ketchup, Cheez-whiz, honey, Nutella, peanut butter, and jelly, as well as easy snacks like graham crackers, regular crackers, cookies, gum, non-chocolate candy, nuts, trail mix, and dried fruit can also be included.

Category C  USD($2000)
Toiletries: Toiletries can provide comfort for many service members. This package can include items such as shaving cream, shampoo, body wash, deodorant, razors, eye drops, toothpaste, dental floss, hand sanitizer, lip balm, sunblock, toothbrush, cough drops, icy hot, and Vicks.

Category D  USD($500)
Health Supplies: This package can include items such as aspirin, bandages, methylated spirit, Pringles, razors, toothbrush, and floss. Snacks, Apple, Oranges, Pasta, Chicken , tootbrush, deodorant


If youre interested in any of the packages, please message us with the category you want and the date of payment, and we will provide you with the necessary information for payment.
